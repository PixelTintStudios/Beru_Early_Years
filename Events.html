Events

At Beru, one of our core practices is celebrating and recognizing the work of our children. We believe in showcasing and honoring each child’s creativity, empowering them to take ownership of their learning and share their journey with parents, educators, and peers. During our events, we proudly display a collection of inspiring artworks, each telling a unique story. Some offer glimpses into the child’s imagination, thought process, and learning, while others reflect their beliefs, emotions, and perspectives of the world around them.

Events and celebrations serve as a meaningful bridge between home and school. These occasions provide rich opportunities for parent involvement and are a powerful way for children to connect with and absorb the symbols and traditions of various festivals.

We are always delighted to have parents join hands with us in various celebrations—singing in the Christmas choir, dancing to the beats of Dandiya, setting up a beautiful Golu display for Dussehra. We also celebrated Grandparents Day with storytelling and their warm participation. Our gallery walks, as well as Berutsov—a celebration of children's achievements in fine and performing arts—are special highlights that honor the children’s learning journeys.

Throughout the year, our calendar is filled with fun, meaningful events, and we’re grateful for the continued support and enthusiasm of our families.
