Welcome To Beru

Beru is a progressive reggio inspired early years school in Bengaluru. At Beru, we strive to nurture a curious, capable and resilient child filled with wonder and knowledge. We strongly believe in providing a supportive and enriching environment to connect parents, educators and the community as a whole.

Environment as a teacher

In line with the Reggio Emilia Approach, we value the environment as the third teacher; the children learn not only from their peers and facilitators, but also from their interactions within the environment.

FAQ

Why are the Foundation years important and how do we cater to that?

Early education matters because early interventions shapes a child's development.At birth, 300 billion brain neurons grow into 1000 trillion connections by age three. Children who start behind often stay behind, thus making early education essential for future success. We here at Beru have programmes designed from 6 months all the way upto 6 years.

How do we support families and Children to settle into a new environment?

We support families and children to settle into Beru by giving them 3 weeks while we are also understanding the child's interests and strengths. During this time, children build relationships with our facilitators, peers and the new environment around them which inturn helps them to come into Beru happy.

Being an open-ended learning environment, how do we cater to the learning rigor?

An open ended environment does not necessarily account for lack of structure. It allows children to deeply engage with materials, ask questions, explore ideas at their own pace. This approach fosters a love for learning while also encouraging critical thinking, problem solving and self directed learning.

What is Reggio and how are we different?

Reggio Emilia Approach is child centered and grounded in the belief that children are capable and curious learners. Children are seen as active participants in their learning journey and the educators act as facilitators guiding exploration based on children's interests.

Will our Beruvians be equipped to transition to a grade school? How?

Absolutely. Our curriculum and approach ensures that children are not only academically ready but also emotionally and socially prepared to transition to a grade school. We achieve this through focussing on skills like problem solving, collaboration, resilience and self regulation.
